Curriculum Vitae

This Curriculum Vitae offers a comprehensive overview of my academic contributions, including research publications, awards, invited talks, teaching, and mentorship. My work centers on the science of empathy and emotion, drawing on psychophysiology, social and cognitive psychology, and interdisciplinary approaches. More than a record of positions and accomplishments, this CV reflects a sustained commitment to advancing scholarship, mentoring the next generation of researchers, and fostering dialogue between science and the broader public.
